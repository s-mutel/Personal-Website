About me:

Hello, I am Sacha Mutel

I am a French traveller and web developper. Before that I graduate in a master of Physiology (training and nutrition). After my master I decided to take my bike and work with around the world, I've been a bike messenger in Paris, Copenhagen and Sydney. During this time I was travelling a lot on my bike, like in Europe, North America, Australia, South-East Asia and Japan. The desire of discovering new places, new cultures, new foods is my main motivation for travelling. I am also looking for new challenges in my sport as well as in my professional life.
